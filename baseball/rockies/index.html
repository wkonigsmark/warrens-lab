The Rockies 60-Minute Master Plan

Phase 1: The Warmup & Mindset

0:00 - 0:08

5m Stretch: Ease in, burn energy, then transition to quiet focus. Listen to the whistle.

3m Huddle: The 3 S's: Safety, Sportsmanship, Success. Focus, Respect, All-Out Effort, and Toughness (No Quitting).

Phase 2: Diamond Dogs

0:08 - 0:15

Full team sprints around the bases starting at home. Crucial: Boys must cheer their teammates to earn their turn to run.

Phase 3: Tactical Throwing & Pop-ups

0:15 - 0:25

Throwing: Pairs at 15ft. Mechanics check: "Warrior Yoga Pose" (ball behind), step to target, follow through.

Catching: Break into coach-led pods. Progress from lobs to "Big Sky" fly balls to build early confidence.

Phase 4: Station Rotations (25m Total)

Station 1: Foundations

Full Hour Support

Small group focused instruction (max 1:4) for players needing extra work on fundamentals: catching, fielding, and proper throwing motion.

Station 2: Force Out Logic

10m Rotation

Situational fielding at SS and 2B. Focus on bag coverage at 2nd and accurate throws for force-outs at 1st. Coach dictates the play target mid-roll.

Station 3: Power & Hip-Turn

10m Rotation

Swing mechanics: Turning hips (Williams) and bat fanning (Gwynn). Drill seargent push-ups for "Long Ball" strength.

Phase 5: The Closing Ceremony

0:50 - 1:00

Review core philosophy. Distribute Baseball Cards (Rewards). Final huddle break...
