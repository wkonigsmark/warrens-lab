Brewers 5U Practice Plan

Phase 1: Warmup & Focus

0:00 - 0:08

5m Stretch: Ease in, get sillies out, then transition to quick team huddle.

Coach Note: 1 coach should lead the stretching, other coaches should consolidate gloves and equipment and setup for the diamond dogs drill (just need 4 bases laid out).

Phase 2: Diamond Dogs

0:08 - 0:15

Full team sprints around the bases starting at home. Crucial: Boys must cheer their teammates to earn their turn to run.

Coach Note: There should be a first base coach and a third base coach. These coaches should be watching very closely how the boys run the bases. If they are not rounding as they go, the coaches need to find time to instruct them. The boys have to touch every base including home; if they do not, their times are not recorded.
